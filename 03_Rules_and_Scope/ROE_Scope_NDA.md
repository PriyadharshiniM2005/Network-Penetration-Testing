# Rules of Engagement (ROE), Scope & NDA

## Rules of Engagement
Defines:
- Allowed systems
- Testing techniques
- Time window
- Emergency contacts
- Reporting process

## Scope
Defines in-scope and out-of-scope assets.

Example:
- In Scope: 192.168.1.0/24
- Out of Scope: Production systems

## NDA
Ensures confidentiality of data and findings.
